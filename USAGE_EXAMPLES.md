### Workflow 1: Building a Custom Prompt
You're in ChatGPT and want to create a technical blog post:

1. Click Prompt Drawer icon
2. Click "Act as a technical writer" (Role)
3. Click "Use a professional tone" (Mood)
4. Click "Use markdown formatting" (Format)
5. Type your specific request
6. Submit!

Result: "Act as a technical writer. Use a professional tone. Use markdown formatting. Write a blog post about Docker containerization best practices."

### Workflow 2: Quick Style Changes
Switching between conversation styles:

**For explanations:**
- "Explain like I'm 5" + "Show your reasoning step by step"

**For quick answers:**
- "No fluff!" + "Be concise and direct"

**For creative content:**
- "Be creative and playful" + "Act as a copywriter"

### Workflow 3: Saving Frequently Asked Questions
You often ask AI to format meeting notes:

1. Highlight your typical prompt: "Please format these meeting notes with action items in bold, decisions in italics, and create a summary at the top"

3. Now available in Saved Prompts dropdown for instant reuse!

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Placeholder Brackets
Create flexible snippets with placeholders:
- "Act as a [ROLE]" - Quick replace ROLE with your need
- "Explain [TOPIC] to a [LEVEL] audience"
- "Generate [NUMBER] examples of [CONCEPT]"

### Tip 2: Chain Multiple Snippets
Build complex prompts by clicking multiple snippets:
1. "Act as a data scientist"
2. "Output as JSON"
3. "Show your reasoning step by step"
Then add your specific question.

### Tip 3: Create Style Presets
Save complete style guides as full prompts:

Example: "Writing Style Guide: Use active voice, short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max), include practical examples, avoid jargon, explain technical terms when necessary, use headers to organize content."

### Tip 4: Context-Specific Collections
Organize by project or use case:

**For Code Review:**
- "Act as a senior software engineer"
- "Focus on security and performance"
- "Suggest improvements"

**For Creative Writing:**
- "Be creative and playful"
- "Use vivid descriptive language"
- "Show, don't tell"

### Tip 5: Leverage Usage Statistics
The drawer learns what you use most. After a week:
- Your top 3 snippets are instantly visible
- Rarely-used snippets sink to bottom
- No manual organization needed!
